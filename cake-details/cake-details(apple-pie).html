cinnamon vanilla sauteed apple covered with flaky puff pastry

AVAILABLE SIZES
2 lbs 2,500.-

(Tea time 6-8 serves, after meal 8-10 serves)

CARE GUIDE

Can be kept at room temperature and travels well for long journeys.

3-5 days in refrigerator or 2 weeks in freezer(please store in airtight container)
*consumed on the day of receipt for the best crispness, as the apple filling may soften the pastry over time.

REHEAT GUIDE

1. preheat oven to 150 c
2. bake for 6-8 minutes with fan on
